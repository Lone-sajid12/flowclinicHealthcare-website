About the Innovation

FlowClinic healthcare is a data analytics solution designed for small and mid-size clinics. It analyzes everyday operational data such as patient arrival times, appointment slots, waiting duration, and staff availability to identify inefficiencies and provide clear, actionable insights.

Important: FlowClinic does not access, store, or analyze any medical records, diagnoses, prescriptions, or patient health information. The platform is built with privacy-first and ethical design principles.
